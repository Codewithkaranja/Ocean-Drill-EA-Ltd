# Ocean Drill (EA) Ltd.

Ocean Drill (EA) Ltd. is a specialized drilling and geotechnical services company operating across East Africa. We provide high-quality, reliable, and efficient drilling solutions for water supply, geotechnical investigation, environmental studies, construction projects, and mineral exploration.

## What This Repository Contains
This repository includes:

- Company branding (logos, design assets)  
- Website source code & updates  
- Project documentation  
- Technical resources and deployment files  
- Marketing and profile materials  

## About the Company
Ocean Drill (EA) Ltd. stands out for its commitment to delivering safe, accurate, and cost-effective drilling services. With skilled professionals and modern equipment, we support both private and public sector projects, ensuring long-term value and sustainable solutions.

## Key Services
- Borehole Drilling & Water Well Development  
- Pump Installation & Maintenance  
- Geotechnical Drilling & Soil Investigation  
- Environmental Drilling & Monitoring  
- Hydrogeological Surveys  
- Site Analysis & Reporting  

## Mission
To provide dependable drilling solutions backed by innovation, professionalism, and integrity while improving access to sustainable water and geological insights across East Africa.
