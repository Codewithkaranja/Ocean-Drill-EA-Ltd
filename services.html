Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and processes

How long does a typical borehole drilling project take?

The duration depends on various factors including depth, geology, and site conditions. Typically, residential boreholes (50-100m) take 3-7 days, while deeper or more complex projects may take 1-3 weeks.

What is the success rate for finding water?

With our comprehensive hydrogeological surveys, we achieve a success rate of over 90% in locating viable water sources. Our advanced surveying techniques significantly reduce the risk of dry boreholes.

Do you offer maintenance services after installation?

Yes, we provide comprehensive maintenance packages including regular inspections, pump servicing, water quality testing, and emergency repair services to ensure your system operates optimally for years to come.

What are the benefits of solar pumps over electric pumps?

Solar pumps offer significant cost savings (up to 60% on operational costs), require no grid connection, have minimal maintenance needs, provide energy independence, and are environmentally friendly with zero emissions.

Do you handle the necessary permits and regulations?

Yes, we manage the entire permitting process including Water Resources Authority (WRA) permits, NEMA approvals for EIAs, and any other regulatory requirements, ensuring your project is fully compliant with Kenyan regulations.
